My Projects

The virtual zoo is a 3D serious game that combines learning with fun and interaction. Players can explore a realistic zoo environment and click on animals using raycasting to display interesting facts and play unique animal sounds. A ticket system at the entrance adds realism, making it feel like an actual zoo visit. The game uses colorful visuals, animal sounds, and interactive elements to keep players engaged while learning.
